School Church

was established in 1978

Church and school are two of the biggest parts of my life. My first memories are going to church and school, because that is all I've ever focused on. I don't play sports, so I spend my free nights after school and weekends studying for tests and going to church.



school library

Was built in 1980

In my school library, we have various types of books- fictional, non-fictional, literature, books on general knowledge, etc. I love to read storybooks and books on general knowledge which have colourful illustrations. Books in my school library are arranged in such a way that students can find them easily.





school auditorium

Was built in 1982

An auditorium is the right place for any social, cultural, and educational activity in a school. An auditorium is also known as an assembly hall. It is an essential part of a school as it has multiple uses to organize celebrations and recreational activities.
